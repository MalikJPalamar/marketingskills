## Strategic Premise

Centaurion occupies a category that doesn't yet have a name in search. The content strategy has one primary job: **build the vocabulary before competitors do**. Every piece of content either defines a term, demonstrates the methodology, or proves the thesis with evidence from the real world. Ownership of the language = ownership of the category.

## Content Production Notes

### Voice Calibration
Per the product-marketing-context voice guidelines: technical precision + first-principles clarity. Every post should feel like it was written by a systems engineer who reads philosophy. Avoid marketing jargon. Use the approved vocabulary.

### Content Proof Protocol
Every claim must be:
1. Grounded in the framework (cite the Three Laws, Fitness Equation, or Sensing Layers), OR
2. Backed by a case study result, OR
3. Sourced from an academic or credible external reference

This is not optional — it's the E-E-A-T foundation.

### Distribution Channels (per piece)
1. Publish to centaurion.me
2. Cross-post key sections to LinkedIn (with canonical link)
3. Thread version on X/Twitter (for Agentic Engineering content)
4. GitHub README update if applicable (links back to web version)
5. Share in relevant Slack/Discord communities (AI builders, technical leaders)
