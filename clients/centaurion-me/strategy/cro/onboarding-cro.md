## Onboarding Contexts

Centaurion has three distinct onboarding experiences, each with different activation milestones:

| Context | New "User" | Activation Milestone | Time to Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Newsletter subscriber | New email subscriber | Reads first issue + clicks | Day 1 |
| Advisory client | New pilot engagement | First executive deliverable shared | Day 7 |
| Course student (Phase 2) | New enrollment | Completes Module 1 + takes assessment | Day 1-3 |

---

## Onboarding 1: Newsletter Subscriber

### Activation goal: Read the first issue and understand the value proposition

**Welcome email (immediate)**:
```
Subject: You're in. Here's what to expect.

[First name],

Every week: one prediction error caught in a live system. What the error revealed. 
How the model updated.

No AI hype. No consulting theater. Just signal from the field.

While you wait for the first issue — the full framework is at centaurion.me/methodology.
It's published because we have nothing to hide.

— Malik

P.S. The 11 Levels of Agentic Engineering assessment takes 3 minutes. 
What level are you? → centaurion.me/agentic-engineering/assessment
```

**Email 2 (Day 3)**: Deliver the lead magnet if they came via a content upgrade, or send "Most read" methodology piece
**Email 3 (Day 7)**: Introduce the Three Laws — first substantive methodology content
**Email 4 (Day 14)**: AOB case study preview — proof the framework works in production

### Activation signal
A subscriber is "activated" when they: (1) open 3+ emails, (2) click at least one link, (3) visit centaurion.me at least twice within 30 days.

## Onboarding 2: Advisory Client (Pilot Engagement)

### Activation goal: Complete Week 1 of the 30-day pilot with clarity and momentum

**Day 0: Engagement kickoff**
- Send signed contract + onboarding packet
- Onboarding packet includes: pilot scope doc, systems audit template, what to expect in each week
- Calendar invites for all 4 weekly check-ins + final presentation

**Day 1: Welcome + context gathering**
- Send: "Before our first session, please complete this context doc"
- Context doc: current AI tools, key stakeholders, what a successful pilot looks like to you
- Goal: arrive at Week 1 call with maximum context, no time wasted on basics

**Week 1 milestone: Systems audit delivered**
- Deliverable: client-systems-map equivalent for their organization
- Establishes baseline — everything from here is measured against this

**Week 2 milestone: AI readiness assessment**
- Score across 5 dimensions (data, people, process, tooling, governance)
- Identifies quick wins vs. structural gaps

**Week 3 milestone: Recommendations + quick wins executed**
- At least 1 quick win deployed by end of Week 3
- This is critical — clients who don't see any change by Week 3 churn

**Week 4 milestone: Executive workbook + dashboard delivered**
- 5-tab executive workbook
- Interactive HTML vital signs dashboard
- 90-day implementation roadmap
- Pilot presentation delivered to client leadership

### Churn signal during pilot
If a client misses 2+ weekly check-in responses without rescheduling, initiate re-engagement immediately: "I want to make sure this engagement is delivering value. What's one thing I can do this week that would make this pilot feel worth it?"

## Onboarding 3: Course Student (Phase 2)

### Activation goal: Complete Module 1 and take the level assessment within 48 hours of purchase

**Immediate post-purchase**:
- Access granted instantly
- Welcome email within 5 minutes: "Here's your login + the one thing to do first"
- "One thing to do first": Take the Agentic Engineering Assessment (if they haven't already) — sets baseline

**Day 1 nudge**:
- "You're [0%] through the Centaurion Method. Module 1 takes 45 minutes."
- Progress bar visible on dashboard

**Day 3 check-in** (if Module 1 not started):
- "The hardest part of any new framework is starting. Module 1 is 45 minutes. Block it on your calendar now."
- Direct link to Module 1, not the course homepage

**Day 7 milestone**:
- Students who reach Module 3 within 7 days have 80%+ completion rate
- Students who haven't started Module 2 by Day 7 have 40% abandonment rate
- Trigger: personal email from Malik if no progress by Day 7

### Completion incentive
Students who complete the course and pass the certification exam get:
- Digital badge (LinkedIn-embeddable)
- Listed in the "Certified Practitioners" directory on centaurion.me
- 30-minute "graduation call" with Malik (scales to group call as cohort grows)
