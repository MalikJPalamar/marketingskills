## Launches on the Roadmap

Centaurion has multiple distinct launchable moments, each targeting a different audience and serving a different role in the funnel:

| Launch | What | Audience | Goal | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **L1** | centaurion.me site + methodology | Existing network | Credibility anchor | Q2 2026 |
| **L2** | Agentic Engineering Assessment Tool | Technical practitioners | Lead gen + email capture | Q2–Q3 2026 |
| **L3** | Original research report | Mid-market executives | Authority + media coverage | Q3 2026 |
| **L4** | Methodology course / framework doc | Broader audience | Revenue + thought leadership | H2 2026 |
| **L5** | Product Hunt | Tech early adopters | Visibility + backlinks | Post-L4 |

## L1: Site Launch — centaurion.me

### Phase 1: Internal (Now)
- Content structure defined (this document + strategy files)
- Product-marketing-context formalized
- Early drafts of methodology and 11 Levels content ready

### Phase 2: Alpha — Soft Launch
**Goal**: Get 20–30 trusted people to read and respond before public announcement.

- Publish methodology hub + 5 glossary terms + homepage
- Share URL personally with: current clients, advisors, peers, conference contacts
- Collect: "Does this land? What's confusing? What's missing?"
- Iterate copy based on feedback, especially the hero and methodology pages

**Email template (personal, not blast)**:
```
Subject: Would you read this and tell me what lands?

[Name] — I've been working on formalizing the Centaurion framework.
centaurion.me is live in early form. Would you spend 10 minutes on
the methodology page and tell me if the argument is clear?

No action required — just honest reaction.
— Malik
```

### Phase 3: Beta — Announce to Network
**Goal**: LinkedIn announcement + first newsletter issue.

- Publish: all 11 Level pages, glossary seed (15 terms), AOB case study (sanitized)
- LinkedIn long-form post: "I've been building in public. Here's the framework."
- First newsletter issue: introduce The Prediction Error brief, link to methodology
- Submit to Hacker News: "Show HN: The 11 Levels of Agentic Engineering"
- Submit GitHub repo to Awesome Lists

### Phase 4: Full Launch
**Goal**: Public announcement with media-ready assets.

- Blog post: "Why Most AI Implementations Are Open-Loop Systems (And What to Do About It)"
- LinkedIn article version of the same
- Email announcement to full list
- Reddit post in r/MachineLearning: "I extended Bassim Eledath's agentic engineering framework to Level 11"
- All pages indexed, sitemap submitted to GSC

## L2: Agentic Engineering Assessment Tool

### What it is
A 10-question self-assessment that outputs: "You're at Level [N]. Here's what's between you and Level [N+1]."

### ORB Strategy

**Owned**: Email capture on results page (required for full report)

**Rented**: 
- LinkedIn post: "Curious what agentic engineering level your team is at? I built a quick assessment." 
- X/Twitter thread: "11 Levels of Agentic Engineering — quick thread + self-assessment"
- GitHub README: add prominent assessment link

**Borrowed**:
- Submit to AI newsletter "swipe file" features
- Pitch to podcast hosts as a pre-interview resource: "have your listeners take the assessment before the episode"

### Launch Checklist
- [ ] Build assessment (Typeform or custom HTML form)
- [ ] Design results pages (one per level or level range)
- [ ] Email capture on full report gate
- [ ] Email sequence triggered on signup (see `content/email-sequence.md`)
- [ ] Share URL in LinkedIn bio, GitHub README, all bylines
- [ ] Announce via newsletter first ("you're getting this before anyone else")

### Success Metrics
- 500 assessments in first 30 days
- 40%+ email capture rate
- 20%+ of email captures book a discovery call

## L3: Original Research Report

### What it is
"The State of AI Transformation in Mid-Market Organizations, 2026"
A survey-based report. 10 questions. 100–200 respondents. Published as PDF + web page.

### ORB Strategy

**Owned**: 
- PDF delivered via email (captures leads)
- Key stats published as ungated web page (SEO + AI citation)
- Data used in multiple blog posts over 6 months

**Rented**:
- LinkedIn: "We surveyed 150 mid-market executives about AI transformation. Here's what surprised us." (5 key stats as carousel)
- Key findings thread on X

**Borrowed**:
- Pitch to 3–5 AI/business media outlets with embargo offer: "Here's the data before it's public"
- Offer to co-brand with a complementary firm (builds their distribution, extends yours)
- Podcast appearances: bring data as talking points

### Launch Sequence
1. Distribute survey (4 weeks before launch)
2. Analyze and write report (2 weeks)
3. Build report landing page + PDF
4. Embargo outreach to media (1 week before launch)
5. Launch: email list gets it first, then public announcement
6. LinkedIn carousel + X thread on launch day
7. Follow-up: "3 things the data got us thinking about" blog post (1 week post-launch)

## L4: Methodology Course / Framework Document

### Product Hunt Launch Plan

**Pre-launch (4 weeks)**:
- Build relationships: engage with Product Hunt community in target categories (AI tools, productivity)
- Prepare listing: tagline, gallery screenshots, 60-second demo video of the framework in action
- Build hunter shortlist: identify 2–3 respected PH hunters to submit (not self-submit)
- Notify email list: "We're launching on Product Hunt in 4 weeks — mark your calendar"

**Launch day protocol**:
- Launch at 12:01 AM PST (maximizes full day exposure)
- Malik responds to every comment personally within 2 hours
- Email list gets "we're live" email at 9 AM PST
- LinkedIn post at 10 AM
- Engage in PH comments, ask questions, spark discussion
- Direct all PH traffic to a dedicated landing page with email capture (not just the main homepage)

**Post-launch (48 hours)**:
- Thank every upvoter with a personal message
- Email list follow-up: share final placement + top comments
- Blog post: "What we learned from our Product Hunt launch"

## Ongoing Launch Cadence

Per the launch-strategy skill: don't rely on one launch. Every content milestone is a micro-launch.

| Milestone | Announcement | Channels |
|---|---|---|
| New blog post | LinkedIn post + newsletter mention | LinkedIn, email |
| New glossary terms added (batch) | "Added 10 new definitions" LinkedIn post | LinkedIn |
| Case study published | Featured email + LinkedIn article | Email, LinkedIn |
| Conference talk booked | Announcement + link to register | All channels |
| New Level page published | LinkedIn thread on that level | LinkedIn, X |
| Newsletter milestone (100, 500, 1000) | Celebration + "here's what I've learned" | All channels |
