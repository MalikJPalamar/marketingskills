# Paid Advertising Strategy — Centaurion.me

**Client:** Centaurion.me (Malik Palamar)
**Phase:** Phase 2 (not Phase 1 — organic and referral traffic comes first)
**Decision framework:** Paid ads for Centaurion are a signal amplifier, not a demand generation engine. They should only run when there is something worth amplifying: a proven organic message, a working landing page, and at least a handful of closed clients who can confirm the positioning is accurate.

---

## Why Paid Ads Are Phase 2

Centaurion.me serves a high-value, low-volume market. A single Pilot Audit ($8.5K–$12.5K) or Fractional CAO retainer ($5K–$8K/mo) changes the economics of paid acquisition significantly — but only once Malik can demonstrate he closes paid leads at a reasonable rate.

**Phase 1 gates that must be crossed before spending on paid:**
1. Homepage hero passes the 5-second test with target ICP
2. At least 2–3 closed clients producing verifiable outcomes
3. Email subscriber list > 500 (proves organic conversion is working)
4. Assessment completion → discovery call booking rate > 15%
5. Monthly organic traffic > 800 sessions

The reason for these gates is not caution for caution's sake. It is that paid ads will pour traffic into a funnel whose conversion mechanics must be proven before you pay to scale them. Running LinkedIn ads into an unproven homepage is expensive waste.

## LinkedIn Ads Strategy

### Why LinkedIn First

Centaurion's ICP is on LinkedIn by professional obligation. They are not hard to find. The challenge is reaching them with a message that doesn't sound like every other AI consulting pitch. Malik's voice — systems engineer who reads philosophy — is differentiated if the ad copy reflects it. Differentiation matters more than targeting precision at the scale Centaurion operates.

### LinkedIn Ad Formats

**For Campaigns 1 and 2 (conversion-focused):** Single Image Ad with Lead Gen Form
- LinkedIn's native lead gen form removes the landing page barrier
- Form fields: First name, Last name, Email, Job Title, Company Name
- Confirmation message: "Malik will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a Discovery Call."
- Follow up immediately (within 4 hours during business days) — LinkedIn lead form leads go cold quickly

**For Campaign 3 (awareness):** Thought Leadership Ad
- LinkedIn's Thought Leadership format runs from Malik's personal profile, not the company page
- Higher credibility, lower CPM, better engagement
- Ideal for sharing frameworks (Three Laws visual, 11 Levels diagram, Fitness Equation)

**For Campaign 4 (retargeting):** Conversation Ad or Message Ad
- Send a personalized InMail to methodology page visitors
- Subject: "You were reading about the Centaurion Method..."
- Keep under 100 words, direct CTA to schedule call

## Retargeting Strategy

### Audience Segments (Built in GA4, Exported to Google Ads)

| Segment Name | Trigger | Retargeting Window | Offer |
|---|---|---|---|
| Methodology Readers | methodology_section_engaged event | 30 days | Discovery call CTA |
| Assessment Starters | assessment_started but not completed | 14 days | "Finish your assessment" |
| Pricing Page Visitors | /work-with-us page view | 21 days | Pilot Audit direct offer |
| Email Subscribers | email_signup event | 60 days | LinkedIn retargeting for brand reinforcement |
| Lead Magnet Downloads | lead_magnet_download event | 45 days | Next step in funnel (assessment or call) |

### Retargeting Ad Principles

1. **Reference what they saw.** "You read about the Three Laws of Centaurion." Acknowledgment builds relevance.
2. **Don't hard-sell.** Retargeting to a sophisticated B2B audience with aggressive "Call Now" language will create negative brand association.
3. **Offer something new.** Methodology readers → offer assessment. Assessment starters → offer completion with a "here's why it matters" hook.
4. **Cap frequency.** Maximum 5 impressions/week per user. Centaurion is not a volume play.

**Google Display retargeting copy (methodology page visitors):**

Headline: The Centaurion Method — Complete Your Assessment
Body: You were reading about the Five Sensing Layers. Find out where your organization stands.
CTA: Take the 5-Minute Assessment
```

```

CTA: Start Here

## Ad Copy Angles Aligned With Centaurion Voice

Malik's voice is distinctive: systems engineer who reads philosophy. The following angles protect against generic AI consulting language.

### Angle 1: The Prediction Error Frame
"Your AI systems are making predictions. Are you measuring the error? Most aren't. That's the starting point."
*Resonates with: Technical leaders who think in feedback loops*

### Angle 2: The Thermostat Problem
"A thermostat responds to its environment. A turbine transforms it. Most AI implementations are thermostats. The Centaurion Method builds turbines."
*Resonates with: Engineering-minded executives who distrust oversimplification*

### Angle 3: The 1,000-Day Window
"The orgs that get human-AI augmentation right in the next 1,000 days will have structural advantages that are expensive to replicate. This is a narrow window."
*Resonates with: Business leaders with competitive anxiety — not fear-mongering, but precise urgency*

### Angle 4: The Diagnostic Frame
"Before we recommend anything, we diagnose. 30 days. 11 levels of agentic capability. A clear picture of where you are, where the gaps are, and what they're costing you."
*Resonates with: Leaders burned by consulting that prescribes before understanding*

### Angle 5: The Systems Engineering Frame
"Human-AI augmentation isn't a strategy problem. It's a systems engineering problem. Most organizations are hiring strategists when they need engineers."
*Resonates with: Technical leaders frustrated by consultants who deliver decks*

## What to Avoid in Centaurion Ad Copy

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|---|---|
| "Leverage AI" | "Build AI systems that compound" |
| "AI-powered" | "Closed-loop agentic systems" |
| "Transform your business" | "Diagnose and rebuild your AI architecture" |
| "Future-proof" | "Structurally advantaged over the next decade" |
| "Unlock potential" | "Reduce prediction error across your sensing layers" |
| "Cutting-edge" | "Built on the 11 Levels framework" |
| Vague superlatives | Specific frameworks and numbers |

The rule: every Centaurion ad should feel like it was written by someone who has actually engineered these systems and finds the consultant-speak embarrassing.
