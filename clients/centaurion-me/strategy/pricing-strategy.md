## Product Mix & Revenue Streams

Centaurion has four distinct monetizable products across the roadmap. Each has a different value metric, buyer, and pricing psychology.

| Product | Type | Value Metric | Buyer | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fractional CAO / Advisory | Services | Time + outcomes | Organizations | Now |
| AI Transformation Pilot | Project | Deliverables + scope | Organizations | Now |
| Workshops | Event | Seats + depth | Teams / individuals | Phase 2 |
| Methodology Course / Certification | Digital product | Knowledge transfer | Practitioners | Phase 2 |

## Stream 1: Advisory Engagements (Primary Revenue)

### Value Delivered
- AI transformation that avoids the open-loop failure mode
- Measurable prediction error reduction + closed-loop architecture
- Executive deliverables (pilot audit, workbook, dashboard, Sankey, governance policy)
- Access to Malik's production-grade exo-cortex as a live proof system

### Next Best Alternative (Price Floor)
| Alternative | Cost |
|---|---|
| Big Four AI transformation practice (Deloitte, Accenture) | $250K–$500K for mid-market |
| Full-time CAO hire | $180K–$280K/year salary + benefits |
| Mid-tier AI consultancy | $80K–$150K per engagement |
| DIY with internal team | Low cash cost, high opportunity cost + high failure rate |

### Pricing Architecture

**Tier 1 — 30-Day Pilot Audit** *(entry point, fixed scope)*
- **Price**: $8,500–$12,500
- **Includes**: Systems audit, AI readiness assessment, 5-tab executive workbook, quick wins roadmap, 90-day implementation plan
- **Value anchor**: Big Four equivalent discovery phase costs $50K+
- **Positioning**: "The diagnostic before the prescription"
- **Who it's for**: Organizations that need to understand where they are before committing to a transformation

**Tier 2 — Fractional CAO Retainer** *(ongoing, recurring)*
- **Price**: $5,000–$8,000/month (3-month minimum)
- **Includes**: 2 advisory sessions/month, async Q&A, AI governance policy, pilot oversight, monthly executive report
- **Value anchor**: Full-time CAO at $200K+/year = $16,700+/month
- **Positioning**: "CAO-level expertise at a director-level cost"
- **Who it's for**: Organizations past the pilot, implementing a sustained AI transformation program

**Tier 3 — Full Transformation Engagement** *(custom scope)*
- **Price**: $25,000–$60,000+ (project-based)
- **Includes**: Full pilot + 90-day implementation + closed-loop architecture build + team training
- **Quote required**: Scope varies significantly by organization size and complexity
- **Positioning**: "From assessment to operational exo-cortex"

### Pricing Psychology Notes
- Always present Tier 3 first on calls — anchors Tier 1 and 2 as accessible
- "Fractional CAO at the price of a junior hire" is the value frame for Tier 2
- Tier 1 is the foot-in-the-door commitment — low risk, high output quality seals the upsell
- Publish Tier 1 pricing openly; keep Tier 2/3 as "starting from" with inquiry CTA

## Stream 2: Workshops

### Format Options
- Half-day: "The 11 Levels of Agentic Engineering" assessment workshop for technical teams
- Full-day: "Designing Your Exo-Cortex" — hands-on architecture session
- Multi-session: "Centaurion Method Implementation" (3 × 2 hours over 3 weeks)

### Pricing

| Format | Group Size | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Half-day workshop | Up to 20 people | $3,500 | In-person or virtual |
| Full-day workshop | Up to 20 people | $6,500 | In-person preferred |
| Multi-session program | Up to 15 people | $12,000 | Includes async support |
| Conference keynote | n/a | $2,500–$5,000 | Speaking fee |

### Notes
- Workshops serve double duty: revenue + pipeline (workshop attendees convert to advisory at high rates)
- Offer one free workshop per quarter to a mission-aligned org (AOB equivalent) as a case study generator
- Record all workshops → repurpose as course content for Phase 2

## Stream 3: Methodology Course / Certification (Phase 2)

### Product Vision
A self-paced course (6–8 hours) covering the Centaurion Method in full, with a certification exam at the end. Priced for individual practitioners, not organizations.

### Pricing Model: Good-Better-Best

| Tier | Name | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | Course Only | $297 | All video lessons, workbook templates, community access (1 year) |
| Professional | Course + Certification | $597 | Everything in Starter + certification exam + digital badge |
| Team | Team License (5 seats) | $1,997 | Professional tier for 5 people, team workshop session |

### Pricing Rationale
- $297 is below the "serious investment" threshold for individual practitioners
- $597 is the certification anchor — the credential is the upgrade driver
- $1,997 team license targets L&D budgets (average L&D per employee: $1,200–$1,800)

### Annual Revenue Projection (Phase 2 target)
- 100 Starter sales/year = $29,700
- 50 Professional sales/year = $29,850
- 10 Team licenses/year = $19,970
- **Total course revenue: ~$80K/year at conservative estimates**

## Pricing Page Design Recommendations

### For Advisory (Primary Page at `/work-with-us`)

**Don't show a pricing table.** Advisory services don't sell from a pricing table — they sell from a conversation.

**Do show**:
- The three engagement types with brief descriptions
- "Starting from $X" for Tier 1 (creates anchor without over-constraining)
- "Custom scope" for Tier 3
- One primary CTA: "Book a Discovery Call" (30 minutes, no commitment)
- Social proof: AOB case study + deliverable previews

### For Workshops (`/work-with-us/workshops`)
Show full pricing table — workshops are transactional and buyers expect to see prices.

### For Course (Phase 2 — `/course` or `/certification`)
Classic three-tier pricing table with:
- Professional tier highlighted as "Most Popular"
- Decoy structure: Starter makes Professional look like obvious upgrade
- Annual subscription toggle if ongoing content updates justify it
- 14-day refund policy prominently displayed

## Pricing Signals to Watch

**Raise prices when**:
- Conversion rate on discovery calls exceeds 50%
- Prospects stop negotiating on Tier 1 pricing
- Waitlist forms and backlog exists for advisory capacity

**Introduce new tiers when**:
- Multiple prospects ask for something that doesn't exist yet
- Workshop demand exceeds available dates
- Course demand research validates willingness to pay

**Current state (Phase 1)**: Pricing is a hypothesis. First 3–5 advisory engagements should test whether the Tier 1 price point generates friction. If no objections, raise by 20%.
